Everything About Samson

I am a Stage Manager, and I have been working in the Twin Cities for the past 12 years. I have worked with many theatres, including Old Log, Six Points, Lyric Arts, and Walking Shadow. In addition to working as a Stage Manager, I have Directed, Designed, Performed, and even wrote almost a dozen produced plays. I am currently working on a program that will hopefully help Stage Managers migrate their work to a digital format. I am also a father to an amazing little boy named Orion, and my unbelievably supportive and wonderful wife and I are expecting our daughter, Theo, in June.
